Hardware and Software

About: Hardware

Hardware refers to the physical components of a computer, electronic device, or system that can be touched, seen, and physically manipulated. In a broader sense, it includes all the tangible devices and equipment used in any technology setup, such as machinery, tools, and electronic circuits. Hardware works together with software, which consists of programs and instructions that tell the hardware how to perform specific tasks.

About: Software

Software refers to the collection of programs, data, and instructions that tell a computer or electronic system how to perform specific tasks. Unlike hardware, which consists of physical components, software is intangible and consists of coded instructions that guide hardware to execute operations.
